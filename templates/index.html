How It Works

Our simple yet powerful process makes buying and selling agricultural produce easier than ever.

1

Register & Verify

Sign up as a farmer or vendor. Farmers undergo a simple verification process.

2

List & Browse

Farmers list their produce. Vendors browse available products and price trends.

3

Order & Negotiate

Vendors place bulk orders and can negotiate prices with farmers up to three times.

4

Fulfill & Rate

Once orders are fulfilled, both parties can rate and review each other's service.
